## Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a complete monitoring system for a viktualierum (dry storage/pantry) that tracks reserve stock items and sends notifications when items are removed. The system uses weight-based sensors configured in on/off mode to detect item presence, providing reliable detection regardless of package type or placement.

**Key Features:**
- Monitors 18 storage locations across 4 shelves
- Detects when any item is removed from storage
- Sends immediate notification to restock
- Works with any original packaging (bags, boxes, jars, cans)
- No ongoing maintenance required
- Total system cost: approximately â‚¬340

**Why This Solution:**
- Most reliable detection method for varied packaging
- Items can be placed anywhere on platform
- No calibration drift or false alerts
- One-time setup, zero maintenance
